The Grand Horizon History

A Legacy of Refined Comfort

Founded in 2012, The Grand Horizon Resort & Spa was built with a singular vision: to create an architectural masterpiece that respects the surrounding environment while providing guests with an unparalleled standard of coastal luxury. Architected by world-renowned ecological visionaries, the resort utilizes local limestone, timber, and glass to construct an environment that feels organic yet breathtakingly modern.

Perched at the highest peak of the Sapphire Peninsula, our resort offers 360-degree views of the indigo waters below. Over the last decade, we have been honored with numerous global design and hospitality awards, including the prestigious *Global Hideaway of the Year*.

But beyond the design and accolades, our true heart lies in our service. The Grand Horizon hosts are trained to anticipate your needs rather than react to requests. We believe in providing an immersive, slow-travel sanctuary where you can pause, breathe, and connect with nature and your loved ones.
